PISCES:
Pisces is one of the three water signs in the zodiac. 
The symbol for Pisces, two Koi fish swimming in opposite directions, represents the Pisces constant variance between fantasy and reality. 
Pisces are known for being highly intuititve, idealistic, and emotional.
